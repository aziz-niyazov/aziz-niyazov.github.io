This page presents the supplementary files of our paper on Interactive Decals:
  • The tested conditions on the study of Decal shape deformation can be accessed from the menu Decal Deformation. We present these results grouped by Decal Shape or Decal Content.
  • The resulting interfaces from the study of Decal constraints can be accessed from the menu Decal Constraints. We show the layouts adaptation to different Physical Object Occlusions and Freeform Display Shapes
  • We also present videos illustrating the behaviour of decals to adapt to object occlusion and user interaction. They can be viewed from the menu Decal Videos
